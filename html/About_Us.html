About us

This webpage intended to spread awareness to Malaysian especially parents on importance of vaccine and how vaccination can really change their kid life. We already living in 2019 with all the healthcare advancement but why the anti-vaccine movement on the rise in Malaysia. According to Johor Health, Environment, Education and Information Committee chairman Ayub Rahmat, the actions of the “anti-vaccine group” was at a worrying level, especially when several diseases, such as tuberculosis (TB), had been on the rise as there was no vaccination since birth. The Health Minister, Dr. Dzulkefly Ahmad said the matter would also be highlighted in the 2019 Budget. Our mission on creating this webpage to educate Malaysian and to stop completely the rise of anti-vaccine movements.

Mission Statement

The purpose of this website is to educate people on the awareness of the importance of vaccine. As the number of anti-vaccine supporter growth gradually year by year , we feel the importance to educate these people hoping that they will realise about the truth and not just getting some fact from unprofesional people.
